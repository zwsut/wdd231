Share Watch Site Plan - WDD131

Share Watch

The name for the site, "Share Watch" was chosen because it will immediately be apparent to visitors that the site's topic is stock tracking. It is a brief and informative name that will tell visitors the site can be used to keep track of stocks they own.

Site Purpose

The purpose of Share Watch is to help users keep track of the value of stocks that they own or are interested in. The site will use an API to display current and accurate information.

Scenarios

  • -What advanced features can help me analyze my holdings and increase profits?
  • -As a beginner, how can I start tracking stocks and not be overwhelmed by information?
  • -How can I fit tracking stocks into my daily routine spending too much time on it?
